About Us

At Foodies, we believe that great food brings people together. Established in 1985, our restaurant is a labor of love, inspired by the rich culinary traditions of Indian and Fast-food. Our team of skilled chefs is dedicated to using only the freshest ingredients, sourced locally whenever possible, to create dishes that celebrate the essence of flavor.

We take pride in our welcoming atmosphere, where every guest feels like family. Whether you are enjoying a cozy dinner for two or celebrating a special occasion with loved ones, our mission is to provide an exceptional dining experience that leaves you wanting more.

Join us at Foodies and discover why we are more than just a restaurant—we are a community hub where food, friends, and fun come together!
